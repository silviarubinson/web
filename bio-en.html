Buenos Aires, 1952.
Psychoanalist and Visual Artist.
She took part of Work Analysis Seminars by Luis Felipe Noé, Eduardo Stupía, and Verónica Gómez. She was trained in the workshops of Juan Doffo, Mirta Kupferminc, Diana Dowek, Leonor Albarellos, Marta Grimberg, Alejandro Wainstein, and Luisa Reisner.
She lives and works in Buenos Aires.
